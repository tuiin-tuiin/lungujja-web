About Us

Lungujja Progressive Nursery and Primary Day and Boarding School is a Christian-based institution committed to offering high-quality education in a nurturing environment.

Our mission is to help children discover their potential, excel academically, and grow spiritually. We aim to cultivate a love for learning that lasts a lifetime, all within the context of Christian teachings and values.

Our vision is to empower every student with the knowledge, skills, and character necessary to succeed in the world and contribute positively to society.

Our Values

Integrity:
We are committed to honesty and fairness in all our dealings.
Excellence:
We strive for the highest standards in education and character development.
Compassion:
We foster an environment of care, empathy, and support.
Faith:
We nurture the spiritual growth of our students, grounded in Christian teachings.
Christian Values:
We integrate Christian teachings in every aspect of our learning, guiding students to live with love, kindness, and respect for others.
Love:
We encourage love for all people, reflecting the love of Christ in our actions and relationships.
Humility:
We teach the importance of humility, recognizing the worth and dignity of others as created in God's image.
Forgiveness:
We emphasize the value of forgiveness and the healing power of reconciliation.
Respect:
We value and respect the diversity of God's creation, fostering an atmosphere of acceptance and mutual respect.
Service:
We inspire students to serve others, living out the example of Christ's selflessness and compassion.
