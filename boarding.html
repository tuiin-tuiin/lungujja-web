Our Boarding Life

A safe, supportive home away from home

Boarding Facilities

At Lungujja Progressive School, we offer a comfortable and secure boarding experience for both day and boarding students. Our boarding houses provide a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to grow academically, socially, and spiritually.

Accommodation

Our boarding facilities are spacious, safe, and well-maintained. Each dormitory is supervised by experienced and caring staff who ensure the safety and well-being of all students. The rooms are furnished with comfortable beds, study tables, and personal storage space.

Meals

We provide nutritious, well-balanced meals that cater to the diverse needs of our students. Special dietary requirements are taken into consideration to ensure that every student enjoys a healthy diet during their stay.

Activities

Boarding students engage in various extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and drama, promoting personal growth, leadership, and teamwork. Spiritual growth is also a priority, with daily devotions and Bible study sessions offered to students.

Discipline & Guidance

We believe in developing strong character alongside academic success. Our boarding system is designed to teach responsibility, respect, and self-discipline. Students are encouraged to take responsibility for their actions, and our staff provide guidance to help them become well-rounded individuals.
