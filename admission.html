Admissions at Lungujja Progressive

Empowering the leaders of tomorrow

Welcome to the Admissions Page

We are thrilled to welcome new students to Lungujja Progressive School! Below, you will find information on how to apply, eligibility requirements, and the admission process.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate and parent/guardian ID
  • Latest school report (for transfer students)
  • Completed medical form (for boarding students)
  • Two passport-sized photographs

How to Apply

To apply, please follow these steps:

  1. Download and complete the admission form from our website.
  2. Submit the form along with the required documents to our admissions office.
  3. Our staff will contact you for an interview or further assessment if necessary.
  4. If your application is successful, you will receive an acceptance letter and further instructions.

Tuition Fees

Our tuition fees are structured to ensure that every child has access to quality education. For detailed information about tuition fees, please contact our admissions office or visit the contact page.
