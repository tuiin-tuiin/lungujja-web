Our Academic Programs

Lungujja Progressive School offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster intellectual growth and prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Nursery School

Our nursery program is tailored to introduce young children to the world of learning in a fun and engaging environment. We focus on foundational skills such as early literacy, numeracy, and social development.

Primary School

Our primary school program emphasizes academic rigor, creative expression, and Christian values. Students are encouraged to develop a strong foundation in subjects like Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities to complement the academic program, including sports, music, arts, drama, and leadership development. These activities help students grow into well-rounded individuals.

Christian Education

As a Christian school, we incorporate biblical teachings into the curriculum, offering students opportunities to explore their faith and develop moral and ethical values rooted in Christianity.
